'Dose' magazine

'Dose' magazine is a fictional publisher focusing on the relationship between drugs and famous artists. Each publication explores different type of stimulants and how they influence cultural scene at the time. This specific edition talks about the effects of LSD on The Beatles and how it changed their life, as well as their music style.

Year of the Ox lucky envelopes

Lucky envelope designs celebrating Year of the Ox, symbolizing strength and determination. I was inspired by the strong connection between Vietnamese farmers to their oxen, working everyday on the field to produce food for the people. The image of oxen looking up to the sky (the sun) hoping for a successful harvest.

Hermanas herBlend coffee tags

herBlend is a special product made by Hermanas by combining different type of coffee beans to bring out the balance between strong taste of Vietnamese beans and fruity, sour flavor of other international beans such as Ethiopia or Panama.

Branding

Pilgrimage

Pilgrimage is a fictional brand specializes in optical eyewear, enhanced with technology to create long-lasting visual-aiding products that can accomodate all type of faces and eye conditions. Each pair is customized to personal preferences, bringing uniqueness to the user.

Influenced by modern self-care lifestyle, accomodated by constant technological evolution, we aim to highlight the importance of vision, one of 5 crucial human senses in survival and development.

Pilgrimage’s logo is a combination between technology (Pilgr) and human’s vision (image). This highlight the brand’s philosophy of optical glasses integrated with technology.

Nokia Podcast

NOKIA Podcast is a Vietnamese lifestyle podcast exploring the stories of two hosts: Linh and Lan, as well as invited guests.

NOKIA is combination of Vietnamese words 'nó' (he/she/they spoken from a person who's older) and 'kìa' (there is it from a distance). Together meaning 'there he/she/them is' with pointing hands.

Manifesto Poster

Based on Tristan Tzara’s 1918 ‘Dada Manifesto’, the manifesto poster was an experiement in converting main ideas and events that happened relating to Dada movement into graphic elements.

Ky Ta by The Nutshell

'Ky ta' is a campaign created by The Nutshell in Saigon, Vietnam, aimed to encourage people to express their opinions and thoughts about beauty standard and unique characteristics on their bodies.

My submissions focuse on the shapes of different parts on the body such as hips or thighs, expressing challenges in trying to fit in clothes that are designed to for certain body types.
