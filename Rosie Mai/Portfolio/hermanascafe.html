Hermanas Cafe

Hermanas is a cafe based in Hanoi, Vietnam focused in bringing high quality coffee to the customers. Their mission is to create a positive, authentic and empowering environment where people can share their stories and find a community within the city, while enjoying locally sourced coffee-based products.

Lucky Envelopes

Lucky envelope designs celebrating Year of the Ox, symbolizing strength and determination. I was inspired by the strong connection between Vietnamese farmers to their oxen, working everyday on the field to produce food for the people. The image of oxen looking up to the sky (the sun) hoping for a successful harvest.
