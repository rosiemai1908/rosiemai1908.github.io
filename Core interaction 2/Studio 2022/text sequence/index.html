INT. LIVING QUARTERS. GALLEY - MOMENTS LATER

OLD sits in a heap of trash. He’s wearing only his sweaty, baggy, mostly unbuttoned union suit, his vest with his watch chain, and his cap. He smokes his PIPE... It goes out.

OLD

Damn.

He tries to find a match in the chaos.

OLD (CONT'D) (to himself)

Fiery pit! Ain’t there no justice left in this world?

He roots through the rubbish, throwing it around... Suddenly, the sound of a MATCH striking (O.S.)! OLD turns around, startled. YOUNG is right behind OLD... with a LIT MATCH... utterly still. Eerily still. He calmly lights OLD’S PIPE. OLD feels a bit uneasy.
