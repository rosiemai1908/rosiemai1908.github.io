YOUNG

*SHUT IT!* I told you I can’t hear no more! *Hold yer jaw!*

OLD

What were it yer accused me of? Y’already told me y’had me figgerd--

YOUNG

I’m tired out of listening to your damned-fool yarns and your Cap’n Ahab horseshit -- you sound like a goddamned parody. Givin’ and nagging orders like a spinster schoolmarm... and... and...

OLD (to himself)

‘Nother conniption fit.

YOUNG (CONT'D)

all-the-while turning this station to the Devil’s own rum hole.

YOUNG (CONT'D)

Well, it’s all horseshit, yer leg, and yer sea life, all of it. And I’m tired of it. If I hear one more word of horseshit coming from your foul, rotten tooth, smelly old mouth––
